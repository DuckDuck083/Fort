Day 1 - Morning Loot, forage, chop trees, craft, and be back by night.
Sack
WASD move. Click to use equipped item. E interacts. 1-5 swaps tools.

What Everything Does

Axe: chop trees/crates and melee zombies.

Pistol: shoot zombies or hunt animals. Press R to reload.

Food/Med: heal. Food also restores energy.

Raw food: goes in your sack. Cook it at the fire with X.

Cooked food: fills hunger and gives a small heal.

Wood: fuel the fire and build base parts.

Scrap: metal used for ammo, traps, rigs, and sentries.

Fuel: powers oil rigs and sentry guns.

Q: add wood to the campfire when near camp.

Crafting table: press E beside the table, then 1-5 to build.

Crafting Table
1 Wall: 10 wood, 2 scrap. Blocks zombie bites at camp.
2 Spike Trap: 8 wood, 3 scrap. Hurts zombies near camp.
3 Food Generator: 12 wood, 4 scrap, 2 cooked food. Makes raw food each day.
4 Oil Rig: 8 wood, 8 scrap, 2 fuel. Makes fuel each day.
5 Sentry Gun: 18 scrap, 3 fuel, 12 coins. Shoots zombies.

Wandering Trader

Quick craft: C wall repair, V ammo, B med pack, F eat cooked food, X cook raw food, Q fuel fire. E opens the crafting table when close.

Last Campfire

Build up your camp by day. Hold the fire line by night.

Use the guide on the left for every item. Open the crafting table at camp to build walls, traps, generators, oil rigs, and sentry guns.
