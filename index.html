What Everything Does

Axe: chop trees/crates and melee zombies.

Pistol: shoot zombies or hunt animals. Press R to reload.

Flashlight: stuns night bosses for a few seconds. It does not kill them.

Rocket: Soldier starts with a rocket launcher. Rockets explode and clear groups.

Food/Med: heal. Food also restores energy.

Raw food: goes in your sack. Cook it at the fire with X, or make stew after crafting a pot.

Cooked food: fills hunger and gives a small heal.

Wood: fuel the fire and build base parts.

Scrap: metal used for ammo, traps, rigs, and sentries.

Fuel: powers oil rigs and sentry guns.

Engineer: starts with an improved sentry and extra fuel.

Q: add wood or a carried fuel barrel to the campfire when near camp.

U: upgrade the campfire to unlock houses, the army fort, and the stronghold.

Crafting table: press E beside the table, then 1-7 to build.

Beginner Guide

  • Move: WASD. Sprint with Shift. Aim with the mouse.
  • Use tools: click with the hotbar item equipped. 1-7 switches tools.
  • Survive: carry fuel barrels or add wood to the campfire with Q, eat with F, and cook at the fire with X.
  • Gather: chop trees and crates with the axe. Hunt animals for raw food and pelts.
  • Build: press E at the workbench, then number keys to build walls, traps, generators, oil rigs, sentries, chests, and a cooking pot.
  • Boss nights: on certain nights an unkillable boss hunts you. Use the flashlight to stun it and run.
  • Upgrade: stand near the fire and press U to upgrade the campfire. Higher campfire levels reveal houses, the army fort, and the stronghold.
  • Trade: wandering traders buy and sell supplies. The pelt trader visits camp every few days and trades pelts for upgrades.
  • Diamonds: stronghold runs can drop diamonds. Spend diamonds here before a run to buy a class.
  • Co-op: turn it on before starting to bring three AI campmates who fight, heal, and help keep the fire alive.
  • Classes: buy classes with diamonds. Soldier starts with the rocket launcher.
